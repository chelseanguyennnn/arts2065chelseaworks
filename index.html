About Me

This is Chelsea (Hoang Lan) Nguyen, a dreamer translating thoughts and philosophies into moving visuals through digital arts.

Projects

Project 0: a piece of mind

A short film that gives voice to a girl who misses home, who feels homesick, who is “nhớ nhà.”

Project 1: Memory Travel

This project explores the question: “What will AI do to us, humans, if we continue exploiting it as we do today?”

The first piece is an essay debating the ethics of using AI in academic environments and its potential impacts. The second is a speculative video history of AI, created using AI-generated images, videos, and sounds, and edited in Adobe Premiere Pro.

Project 2: The Archive of the After

This speculative project imagines “The Archive”, a decentralised network where people around the world store, share, and protect their collective knowledge. In this future, information is free, but truth becomes more complex.

ARTS2065 - Screen Production II

Week 1: Camera exercise

This is a camera exercise that practices point-of-view (POV) shooting. The short sequence shows a young man bursting into a classroom and grabbing his water bottle in desperation, only to find that it’s completely empty.

Week 2: Transformative photo

This is a transformative photo exercise that begins as a still image and gradually unfolds like a film, intentionally tricking the audience at first. The sequence does not have a specific narrative but simply explores the inner world of a young girl studying music.

Week 3: Stalking exercise

This is a stalking exercise. The sequence follows a boy whose blurred world turns upside down after he loses his glasses. A girl’s simple act of kindness leads to a moment of confusion and misunderstanding.

Week 4: Shot-reverse-shot

This is a SHOT-REVERSE-SHOT exercise.
